About Taipei New East Rotary Club

Founded in 1987, the Rotary Club of Taipei New East belongs to Rotary International District 3522. Members come from diverse professional backgrounds, engaging in weekly luncheon meeting, community service, international service, vocational service, and youth service, with ongoing commitment to education, social welfare, and international exchange.

What is Rotary?

Founded in 1905, Rotary is a global service organization that brings together professionals and leaders from all walks of life, united by the core principle of 'Service Above Self.' Through fellowship and collaboration, Rotary members improve communities and promote world peace. Rotarians around the world drive service projects addressing health, education, environment, and economic development, while building genuine friendships and upholding high ethical standards in their professional and personal lives.

Service Highlights

From rural education to international cooperation, we put the Rotary spirit into action

Rural STEM Education

Bringing Maker Education & AI Resources

Introducing STEM maker education and AI resources to Tongmen Elementary in Hualien, Luodong Senior High in Yilan, Lanyu Senior High, and Magong Senior High in Penghu

International Cooperation

35 Years of Sister Club Friendship with Hiroshima

Partnered with Hiroshima Southwest Rotary Club since 1991, collaborating on international service projects spanning Mongolia, Cambodia, Japan, and Taiwan

Education Support

Library & Facility Improvements for Rural Schools

Supporting Tafalong tribal library in Hualien, Yuemei Elementary food & farming education and dance classroom, Mingli Elementary library system, and Ruifang Junior High music classroom equipment in New Taipei City

Youth Service

Youth Leadership & Career Education

Youth 3-on-3 basketball tournament, Rotaract mentorship, and youth career exploration

Social Welfare

Pioneering Charitable Initiatives

Seed donations and long-term support for United Way, Child Welfare League, and other social welfare organizations

Community Beautification

Community Landscape & Environmental Projects

Yangmingshan Park cherry blossom garden, Liberty Square Yunhan Pond cherry blossom donation, Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall LOHAS garden and succulent plant garden donation
